GENERAL INFORMATION

Greece & the Greek Islands are famous for 3 things: ancient civilization, amazing landscapes, and well-established tourism.

Really beloved by people, Greece is so rich in culture and beauty that it never seems to be completely explored, no matter how many times you visit it. After all, this is one of the reasons that brought it in the list with the top 20 tourist holiday destinations worldwide.

Travel to Greece and the islands have become a must activity, according to the modern lifestyle. Over the last decades, it has been visited by the jet-setters, including politicians, musicians, actors, artists and other celebrities with a significant contribution to the popularity of the country.
